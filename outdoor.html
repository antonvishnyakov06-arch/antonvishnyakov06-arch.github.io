Explore the beautiful Finnish nature with an experienced guide

Escape the ordinary and experience the beauty, silence, and raw power of Finnish nature just minutes from Helsinki. Our outdoor tours and adventures take you deep into the forests, across frozen lakes, and through the stunning archipelago, offering authentic experiences in every season.

Summer & Autumn Adventures

When the forests are alive with color and the days stretch long, we invite you to:

  • Hike through Nuuksio, Sipoonkorpi, and other nearby national parks
  • Cycle along peaceful forest trails and scenic countryside routes
  • Discover the coastal beauty on archipelago tours
  • Forage for wild herbs, berries, and mushrooms with expert guides
  • Paddle across calm lakes and coastal waters by canoe or kayak

Cook and enjoy traditional Finnish meals over an open fire in the wild.

Winter Adventures

As snow blankets the landscape, Finland transforms into a peaceful arctic wonderland. Join us for:

  • Snowshoeing through frozen forests and snow-covered national parks
  • Cross-country skiing lessons for beginners in beautiful natural settings
  • Alpine skiing lessons near Helsinki with personal instruction
  • Ice skating on natural frozen lakes (when conditions allow)
  • Gliding through the snow with a traditional kicksled, fun for all ages

Experience cozy evenings by the fire and, when conditions allow, hunt for the northern lights.
