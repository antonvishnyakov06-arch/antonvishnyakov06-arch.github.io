Finnish Nature

Nature is never far in Finland. From peaceful forests near the capital to the vast, silent landscapes of the north, our outdoor tours bring you into the beauty of Finnish nature. Hike, bike, or simply walk — breathe in the fresh air and feel the calm.

City Tours

Get to know Helsinki with a local guide. Discover its architecture, quirky corners, rich history, and daily rhythms. Our city tours go beyond the sights — we share the stories, traditions, and local perspectives that make Helsinki unique..

Winter Adventures

In Lapland, Northern Finland, and just outside Helsinki, winter is a season of stunning beauty, peaceful silence, and deep connection. Our winter tours offer authentic experiences across the country — from skiing across frozen lakes, snowshoeing through tranquil pine forests, to ice skating on crystal-clear frozen lakes and kick sledding along snowy trails near the city. You’ll discover how people here don’t just endure winter — they embrace and celebrate it. Whether deep in the Arctic wilderness or close to urban life, our tours focus on genuine winter experiences where snowy landscapes invite moments of stillness, joyful activity, and reflection.

Traditional food

Whether it’s enjoying salmon soup in a local market, cooking by the fire in a forest hut, or sipping hot berry juice after a snowy hike — our tours always include a taste of Finland’s culinary traditions. Simple, seasonal, and deeply satisfying.
